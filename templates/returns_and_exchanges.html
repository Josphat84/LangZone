Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your purchase! If you’re not completely satisfied, our returns and exchanges process is simple and hassle-free.

Return Policy

  • Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, in original packaging, and with all tags attached.
  • Final sale items cannot be returned or exchanged.

How to Return or Exchange

  1. Contact Us: Email support@ljshop.com with your order number and reason for return/exchange.
  2. Prepare Your Package: Securely pack your item(s) and include your order confirmation.
  3. Ship It: We’ll provide a prepaid return label. Drop off at your nearest carrier location.
  4. Refunds/Exchanges: Once received and inspected, we’ll process your refund or send your exchange within 3-5 business days.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a return?
Returns are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your item.
Are return shipping costs covered?
Yes, we provide a prepaid return label for eligible returns and exchanges.
Can I exchange for a different item?
Yes, exchanges are available for items of equal value and in stock.
